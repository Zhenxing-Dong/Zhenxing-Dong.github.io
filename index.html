Zhenxing Dong (董振兴)

I am currently a final-year Ph.D. student in Department of Electronic Engineering, Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU), supervised by Prof. Yuye Ling. I also work very close with Prof. Yan Li. Before that, I received the B.Eng. degree in Information Engineering from South China University of Technology (SCUT) in 2020.

My research interests include optics, computational displays, and computational imaging. I primarily work on co-designing optical systems and algorithms towards next-generation VR & AR displays.

I am looking for a research position starting in the summer of 2025. Please reach out if you think I could be a good fit!
