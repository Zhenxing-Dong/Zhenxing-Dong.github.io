Zhenxing Dong (董振兴)

I am currently a fourth-year Ph.D. student in Department of Electronic Engineering, Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU), supervised by Prof. Yuye Ling. Before that, I received the B.Eng. degree in Information Engineering from South China University of Technology (SCUT) in 2020. Additionally, I transfered to be a PhD student in March, 2022.

My research interests include Virtual & Augmented reality, Computational displays & imaging, and Biomedical imaging.
