Zhenxing Dong (董振兴)

I am currently a fourth-year (2020.9-2026.3) Ph.D. student in Department of Electronic Engineering, Shanghai Jiao Tong University(SJTU), supervised by Prof. Yuye Ling. Before that, I received the B.Eng. degree in Information Engineering from South China University of Technology in 2020.

My research interests include Virtual/Augmented reality, Computational displays, Computational imaging, and Biomedical imaging.
